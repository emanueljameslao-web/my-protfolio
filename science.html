Science Project

Renewable Energy: Powering the Future

This project explores how renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydro power are shaping a sustainable future. It highlights how these clean energy solutions reduce pollution and provide long-term benefits to both the environment and society.

The website demonstrates how technology and innovation can work together to make renewable energy more efficient and accessible. Through data visualization and simulations, it educates users on how energy can be harvested and converted into power.

This project was created as part of our school’s science exhibit, aiming to raise awareness about environmental protection and energy sustainability.
